    Burning Process⚓︎

    At the end of the pool, the Burns collector contains a certain amount of tokens that will be deducted from the total supply and discarded forever.

    Since the idea of the project is to produce value, we believe that this autonomous burning process is the greatest way to maintain a good market impact and to prevent token deflation.

    Even though the risk of losing value is likely common in the cryptocurrency world, our algorithms along with a process mainly controlled by holders will help prevent or at least minimize those risks.
